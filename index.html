Carlsbad, California

Carlsbad is a coastal city located in the North County region of San Diego County, California. It is situated 87 miles south of downtown Los Angeles and 35 miles north of downtown San Diego. As of the 2020 census, the city had a population of 114,746. Carlsbad is known for its beautiful beaches, shopping, dining, and much more. It lies along a lagoon on the Pacific Ocean just south of Oceanside, in a winter vegetable- and flower-growing district. The city was named after a famous spa in Karlsbad, Bohemia. The city's history began with the Luiseño people, as well as some Kumeyaay in the La Costa area. Today, Carlsbad is a popular tourist destination and home to many businesses in the golf industry. It truly is a gem on the southern California coast.
